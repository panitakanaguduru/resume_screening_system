**Job Title**: Technical Project Manager  
**Location**: Remote (US-based only)  
**Division**: Product Delivery  

**Job Summary**:  
We are looking for an experienced Technical Project Manager to oversee the planning and delivery of software development initiatives. You will lead cross-functional teams, track deliverables, and ensure projects meet scope, budget, and timeline goals.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Define project scope, timelines, and resources with leadership and clients.
- Use Agile (Scrum/Kanban) frameworks to manage sprints and daily standups.
- Maintain project documentation, risk registers, and sprint boards (Jira).
- Act as the main point of contact between tech, QA, and business teams.
- Ensure timely delivery and manage scope changes or blockers.
- Conduct stakeholder updates and sprint retrospectives.

**Required Skills**:
- 3+ years managing software development projects.
- Hands-on experience with project tools like Jira, Confluence, or ClickUp.
- Familiarity with SDLC, Agile, and Waterfall methodologies.
- Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills.

**Preferred Qualifications**:
- PMP or Certified Scrum Master (CSM) certification.
- Background in software engineering or product development.
- Experience with resource planning and project cost estimation.
