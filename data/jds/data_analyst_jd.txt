**Job Title**: Data Analyst  
**Location**: Remote  
**Industry**: Healthcare Analytics  

**Job Summary**:  
We are seeking a Data Analyst to support our decision-making processes by collecting, analyzing, and interpreting complex datasets. The role will focus on improving healthcare outcomes and operational efficiencies through insightful analysis.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Collect, clean, and validate data from SQL databases, Excel files, and APIs.
- Design and maintain automated dashboards using Power BI or Tableau.
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and statistical testing.
- Support A/B testing and build reports for campaign performance.
- Present findings to cross-functional teams and provide actionable recommendations.
- Work with data engineers to improve data pipelines and models.
- Document metrics, processes, and data definitions in a central knowledge base.

**Required Skills**:
- Advanced SQL skills (joins, subqueries, window functions).
- Proficiency in Python or R for statistical computing.
- Hands-on experience with Power BI, Tableau, or similar tools.
- Strong analytical and storytelling skills.

**Preferred Qualifications**:
- Familiarity with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP.
- Experience working in healthcare, insurance, or public health datasets.
- Knowledge of predictive modeling and regression analysis.
